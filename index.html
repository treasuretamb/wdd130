Gauteng, South Africa

Gauteng is a large continent that is home to two major cities in South Africa, Johannesburg (The city of gold) and the capital city Pretoria also known as the City of Tswane. It is located right in the middle of the country meaning it is bordered by all provinces. Gauteng is known for it's diversity and business, it draws many people because of the opportunities available. It also has a town in Johannesburg that is the richest square mile in Africa and that has the tallest building in Africa, Sandton.
